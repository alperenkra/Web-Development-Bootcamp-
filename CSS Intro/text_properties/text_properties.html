Suluova

Suluova is a town in Amasya Province of Turkey, located on high ground inland within the central Black Sea region . It is the seat of Suluova District.[2] Its population is 39,286 (2021).[1] Altitude is 510 m. The mayor is Fatih Üçok (AKP).

Social Life

Name Suluova was known in antiquity as KuluArguma, meaning 'watery' .[citation needed] This was later translated into Turkish first as Suluca and later as Suluova, meaning watery plain. Two-thirds of the district lies on the plain (watered by the Tersak an River), one-third in the surrounding mountain forest.
